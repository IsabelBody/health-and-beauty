# Blush reads orange / muddy — want cool pink–red

Current liquid blush(es) pull **too orange** and **slightly muddy** on deep winter / cool skin. Placement above cheekbone can stay; **shade undertone** is wrong.

## Target replacement

- **Cool pink–red** family: rose, berry, cool red, raspberry — not peach, coral, warm orange, or brick
- Aligns with [[../me/face-blueprint]] (deep winter)
- Enough colour to avoid flat face when foundation is on — without fighting eyes
