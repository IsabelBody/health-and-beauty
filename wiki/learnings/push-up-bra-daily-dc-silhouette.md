# Push-up bra daily — fuller chest read (DC triangle)

**One-line rule:** wear a **push-up bra every day** under tanks and corsets — it reads as a fuller chest, which helps the **shoulder-up triangle** whether or not cleavage is visible.

## Evidence

Push-up lifts and shapes the bust so the upper body looks proportionally fuller. That supports the DC target silhouette (slightly widest at shoulders/neck) even in structured tanks with high necklines — not only on cleavage-forward looks.

## Implication

- Default undergarment for the [[../../areas/style/daily-uniform-miniskirt-dc]]: push-up, not a neutral t-shirt bra, unless a specific outfit requires otherwise.
- Still pair with open necklines when you want eye drawn up; bra effect works with or without visible cleavage.
- Source 1–2 well-fitting everyday push-ups (comfort for all-day wear) — track in [[../../projects/pinterest-miniskirt-board]].
