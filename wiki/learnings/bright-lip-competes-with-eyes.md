# Lip finish and colour must not compete with eyes

Bright or shiny lips pull focus away from eyes (your dominant feature). **Matte lip liner / matte lip** — well coloured but natural — keeps the face anchored.

## Rules

- **Matte only** — see [[matte-lips-anchor-eyes]]
- Avoid gloss and any shine on lips
- Press powder on lip colour for longevity if needed

## Implication

- Default lip step: matte liner + matte colour; supports [[../me/face-blueprint]] priority (brows + eyes first)
