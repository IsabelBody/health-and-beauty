# Matte lips only — eyes are the anchor

Any lip **shine** (gloss, glossy liner, dewy finish) looks unnatural on you and **pulls attention off your eyes**. Without that anchor, the face feels lost in the midface.

**Matte lip liner / matte lip color** works better: well coloured but still natural. Keeps brows + eyes as the dominant focal point per [[../me/face-blueprint]].

## Rules

- Matte only — no gloss, no shiny liner
- Colour can be present; does not need to be nude — just not competing with eyes
- Press powder on lip color if needed for longevity (from earlier testing)
