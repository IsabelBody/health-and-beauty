# Shadow above the fold, not in the crease

On hooded + deep set eyes, dark shadow **in** the natural crease vanishes when you open your eyes and can make eyes look smaller. Depth belongs in the **brow-bone shadow zone** (fake / elevated crease), blended up and out toward the temple.

## Evidence

Confirmed eye shape Jun 2026; matches pro guidance (No7, wikiHow, Charlotta Eve) and your placement notes (outer socket, avoid heavy crease-only dark).

## Implication

- Apply eyeshadow with eyes **open**; check visibility after each step.
- Mobile lid stays lighter; matte depth on outer socket / below brow bone.
