# Hydration is likely the #1 hair issue

Hair reads **dry** and under-hydrated more than anything else right now. Fixing moisture overnight clearly helps morning condition, but dryness and frizz are not the same problem — you can be less dry and still look messy/frizzy.

## Evidence

- Default state: hair looks dry.
- **2026-06-01 overnight:** gloss treatment + hydrating spray, pushed into bonnet → **much better in the morning than usual**, but still **frizzy / messy** looking.

## Implication

- Treat **hydration** as the first lever (overnight seal: gloss or similar + hydrating spray + bonnet).
- Keep researching what reduces **frizz / flyaways** after moisture is improved (technique, product weight, smoothing, cut, etc.).

## Context (profile Jun 2026)

Fine, low-density, straight; lengths **always dry**; frizz **when dry**; scalp balanced. See [[../hair/my-profile]].
