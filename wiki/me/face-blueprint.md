## Feature priority (makeup emphasis order)

1. Brows + eyes  
2. Orbital structure + cheekbones  
3. Nose  
4. Skin contrast / pallor  
5. Lips  
6. Jaw  

## Placement principles (from your blueprint doc)

- Blush: elongated, lifted, outer cheek / toward temples — avoid apple or horizontal center blush
- Blush **color**: cool pink, rose, berry, cool red (deep winter) — avoid orange, coral, muddy warm tones
- Highlight: outer cheekbone, inner corner, soft browbone, cupid's bow — avoid center forehead, under-eye, nose tip
- Concealer: inner under-eye hollow, tear trough, side-of-nose shadow — thin layers, tap not swipe
