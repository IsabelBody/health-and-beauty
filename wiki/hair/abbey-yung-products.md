## Step 1 — Pre-shampoo bond repair

Spray on **dry** hair until saturated; **≥10 min** before wash (can sleep in). She uses **~2 of 3 washes/week** with Epres; alternatives are cream treatments used pre-wash.

| Product | Tier | Notes from Abbey |
|---------|------|------------------|
| **Epres Bond Repair Treatment** | Prestige | Her default pre-shampoo; spray = easy even application |
| Redken Acidic Bonding Concentrate (treatment) | Prestige | Cream; also used post-shampoo |
| L'Oréal Bond Repair Concentrate | Drugstore | |
| Garnier Bond Repair Treatment | Drugstore | ~5 min |
| OGX Bond Protein Repair **Pre-Shampoo** | Drugstore | |
| Not Your Mother's Bond Repair Treatment | Drugstore | |
| Living Proof Triple Bond Complex | Prestige | Listed in part 1 description |

**Weight (bond repair guide video):** Lightest application ease — **Epres**, **Pantene post-wash mist**; middle — K18, Living Proof; heavier — OGX, Redken, L'Oréal creams.

---

## Step 2 — Pre-shampoo oil (optional)

**≥20 min** on lengths/ends; can be overnight. **Skip** on in-shower bond / K18 days. If using Epres: wait 10 min after Epres, then oil on damp hair.

| Product | Best for (Abbey) |
|---------|------------------|
| OGX **Extra Strength Coconut Miracle Oil** (penetrating) | Thick / coarse |
| OGX **Nourishing Coconut Milk** anti-breakage serum | Medium |
| OGX **Coconut Oil Mist** | **Fine / thin** — lightest |
| OGX Argan Oil of Morocco Penetrating Oil | Part 2 list |

**Fine hair (your profile):** start with **Coconut Oil Mist** or serum — not heavy Miracle Oil unless very dry and you rinse well.

## Step 5 — Post-shampoo bond repair (in shower)

On **damp** hair after shampoo; rinse per product. **K18:** ~4 min, **not** same wash as other bond creams; often after clarify. Others: **5–10 min**.

| Product | Notes |
|---------|--------|
| **K18 Leave-In Molecular Repair Hair Mask** | ~every 3 weeks maintenance; 6 washes after colour |
| Redken Acidic Bonding Concentrate | |
| L'Oréal Bond Repair (in-shower treatment) | |
| Garnier **Hair Filler + Bonding** / Bond Repair Treatment | Relish: “Hair Filler + Bonding” |
| OGX Bond Protein Repair (in-shower) | |
| Not Your Mother's Bond Repair Treatment | |

**Skip pre-oil** on these wash days.

## Step 7 — Post-wash bond repair (optional)

Apply post-shower; **Living Proof:** wait **10 min**, blow-dry for activation. **Pantene Pro-Vitamin Essence:** spray by sections; no mandatory wait/heat.

## Her personal “favourites” called out in part 1

- **Clarifying:** Pantene **Sheer Volume** — uses some weeks for all washes  
- **Glossing:** L'Oréal **EverPure Glossing** over Elvive Glycolic Gloss  
- **Pre-shampoo bond:** **Epres** (routine default)  
- **Pre-shampoo oil:** OGX **Coconut Miracle Oil** historically; acknowledges fine hair → **Mist** or **Milk Serum**  
- **Post-wash bond:** Living Proof **Triple Bond Complex**; drugstore alt **Pantene Pro-Vitamin Essence**
