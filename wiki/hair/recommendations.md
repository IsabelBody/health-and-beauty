## Wash-day patterns (use these)

**Frequency:** Every 2–3 days (flexible).

| Wash type | When | Steps |
|-----------|------|--------|
| **Weekly reset** | ~1×/week (every 2 weeks if scalp tight) | OUAI Detox → Dove mask → Pureology leave-in |
| **Standard** | Other wash days | Glycolic Gloss shampoo → Glycolic Gloss conditioner → Pureology leave-in |
| **Detangle support** | Wet combing very hard | OGX argan mist 20+ min → then standard or reset |

**Do not run every Abbey Yung step.** Skip bond repair (1, 5, 7) until damage sources appear. See [[step-rules-and-conflicts]].

## Product weight rules (fine + low density)

- Conditioner, leave-in, pre-oil: **lightweight** (sprays, light serums, milks — not heavy penetrating oils every wash).  
- If lengths feel **waxy or heavy** after adding wash-day mask + overnight gloss: drop one layer, not all hydration.  
- **Double shampoo** optional for thorough scalp cleanse; not required.

## Frizz vs hydration

- Frizz **when dry** → moisture first (conditioner every wash, leave-in, overnight stack).  
- If still messy after consistent hydration → light smoothing sealer or technique (blow-dry downward, brush type) — phase after wash routine stable.

## Isotretinoin

- Expect **drier lengths and possibly scalp** over coming months.  
- Avoid aggressive clarifying + heavy oil + rich mask same week without checking feel.  
- Simplify actives on scalp unless GP advises otherwise.

## Shedding concern

Products do not replace medical workup. Track shedding; discuss with **GP** if increased, sudden, or with visible thinning. Note iron supplement plan in [[../../areas/skincare/medications]].
