# Hair step rules and conflicts

Quick rules when combining [[abbey-yung-method]] steps — prevents overload and “greasy but still dry” outcomes.

## Non-negotiables (Abbey’s core)

1. **Cleanse scalp properly** (right shampoo/deep-clean for *your* scalp).  
2. **Condition every wash** (at least one of conditioner, mask, or gloss that works as conditioner for you).  
3. **Protect lengths** post-wash (leave-in; heat protectant if using hot tools).

## Do not stack blindly

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Not every bond-repair slot every wash** | Pre + in-shower + post + leave-in bond repair is optional layering, not required |
| **Pre-oil vs in-shower bond repair** | She skips pre-oil on days with in-shower bond repair (oil can interfere with penetration) |
| **Pre-oil + post-wash bond repair** | Avoid same wash — can feel heavy/greasy ([WWD](https://wwd.com/pop-culture/culture-news/abbey-yung-hair-method-1238324567/)) |
| **K18 same wash** | Do not mix K18 with other bond treatments that wash; often solo bond day |
| **K18 prep** | Often after clarifying/deep-clean per K18 guidance; skip pre-oil that day |
| **One bond “hero” per wash** | Pick pre *or* in-shower *or* post — build up only if hair tolerates |

## Pre-shampoo decision tree

```
Need bond repair this week?
├─ Yes, pre-shampoo spray (e.g. Epres) → wait ≥10 min
│   └─ Also need oil?
│       ├─ Yes, no in-shower bond today → oil ≥20 min (can be overnight) → wash
│       └─ No → wash
└─ No
    ├─ Dry / tangled → pre-oil only (≥20 min) → wash
    └─ Fine → skip to wash; use in-shower bond or condition only
```

**Pre-shampoo bond + pre-oil + in-shower bond:** She sometimes does pre bond + oil when **no** in-shower bond that day. Pre bond + in-shower bond (no oil) is another valid path.

## In-shower order (typical)

1. Wet hair  
2. Shampoo (clarify and/or regular — one or two passes; your choice)  
3. Medicated spot treatment if applicable  
4. Post-shampoo bond repair (if this wash) — rinse per product  
5. Conditioner / mask / gloss — leave on minutes  
6. Rinse  

## Post-wash order

1. Post-wash bond repair (if this wash) — wait ~10 min  
2. Towel dry / microfiber — gentle  
3. Leave-in + heat protectant  
4. Style (steps 9–10)  

## Between-wash vs wash-day

Overnight oil/gloss/spray (step 11) can complement wash-day hydration. If lengths feel **coated** after adding in-shower mask + overnight oil, reduce one layer.

## Experiment protocol (this repo)

- Change **one** step or product per experiment window (see [[../../areas/hair/experiments]]).  
- Log scalp at 24 h and 48 h; lengths same day and next morning.  
- If greasy + dry coexist → suspect overload or wrong shampoo weight, not “need more oil.”
