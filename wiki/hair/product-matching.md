# Hair product matching (Abbey Yung framework)

How to **choose** products within the [[abbey-yung-method]]. Shampoo follows **scalp**; conditioner, mask, leave-in follow **strand thickness** (and tolerance for weight).

## Three axes

| Axis | What it means | Drives |
|------|----------------|--------|
| **Scalp type** | Oily, dry, balanced (+ medicated if flakes/itch) | Shampoo (steps 3, 4, 4b) |
| **Strand thickness** | Fine, medium, coarse — diameter of one strand | Conditioner, mask, leave-in, pre-oil weight |
| **Density** | How much hair on head | Amount of product, not always formula |

**Texture** (straight, wavy, curly) is secondary to thickness for product weight in this framework.

## Scalp type → shampoo

| Scalp | Signs | Shampoo direction | Clarifying |
|-------|--------|-------------------|------------|
| **Dry** | Tight, flaky, rarely oily | Gentle, hydrating; avoid harsh frequent clarify | Occasional only; every 2+ weeks if at all |
| **Oily** | Greasy within 24–48 h | Effective cleanse; balancing/detox options | Can use deep-clean more often — still condition lengths |
| **Balanced** | Minimal issues | Most gentle daily shampoos | Clarify when buildup (dull, heavy, products stop working) |
| **Flaky / itchy** | Needs actives | Medicated (e.g. zinc pyrithione, ketoconazole) — see GP if persistent | May alternate with regular; not necessarily daily for whole head |

**Rule:** Pick everyday shampoo from **scalp behaviour**, not how lengths *look*.

**Wash frequency:** When scalp needs it (every 1–2 days to ~2×/week) — not “training” longer gaps.

## Strand thickness → conditioner & leave-in

| Thickness | Conditioner / mask | Pre-shampoo oil | Leave-in |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Fine** | Lightweight, hydration/slip; avoid heavy oils on lengths | Lightest formats (e.g. mist-style oils) | Light spray; avoid buildup |
| **Medium** | Balanced moisture | Serum-weight oils | Moderate leave-in |
| **Coarse** | Richer masks, more slip | Heavier penetrating oils tolerated | Richer creams acceptable |

**Application:** Mostly **mids to ends** unless scalp tolerates otherwise. Conditioner is not the primary scalp treatment.

## Clarifying / deep-clean — when

Use as **reset**, not default daily (unless your scalp genuinely needs that cleanse power every wash — then still condition well).

**Signals you need a reset:**

- Hair feels heavy right after wash  
- Products “stop working”  
- Dull, waxy, or inexplicably greasy lengths  
- Lots of dry shampoo / styling buildup  
- Hard water (chelating shampoos)

**Frequency starting point:** ~1×/week; dry or colour-treated → every 2 weeks; adjust from scalp feedback.

## Bond repair — when

For **colour, bleach, heavy heat** damage — optional paths:

- Pre-shampoo spray (e.g. Epres-style)  
- In-shower after shampoo (cream treatments)  
- Post-wash leave-on (K18-style protocols)  

**Not required** in every slot. See [[step-rules-and-conflicts]].

## Between-wash (step 11)

| Need | Options |
|------|---------|
| Scalp oil between washes | Dry shampoo, refresh sprays |
| Length dryness | Overnight oil, gloss, hydrating spray + protection (bonnet/silk) |

Align with [[../../areas/hair/routine-between-washes]].
