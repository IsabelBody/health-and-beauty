## Summary

Fine, low-density, straight hair with **balanced scalp** but **always-dry lengths**, **frizz when dry**, and **frequent buildup**. Very hard wet detangling. Primary goals: **hydration** and **frizz**. No colour or heat currently; may blow-dry daily later. Starting **isotretinoin 5 mg** (1 Jun 2026) — expect increased dryness over time.
