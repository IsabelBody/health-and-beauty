## Usage rules (fine + low density)

- **Reset day:** OUAI Detox → Dove mask 3–5 min (mids to ends) → Pureology leave-in (2–4 sprays).
- **Standard day:** Glycolic Gloss shampoo → Glycolic Gloss conditioner 3–5 min → Pureology leave-in (light).
- **Detangle day:** OGX argan mist on dry lengths 20+ min → standard or reset wash; **no** pre + post oil same day.
- If lengths feel waxy: drop OGX or Dove mask frequency before dropping conditioner or leave-in.
