# Hair experiments

Track **one change at a time** (Abbey Yung adoption rule). Link each run to a `journal/` entry; promote stable insights to `wiki/learnings/`.

**Framework:** [[../../wiki/hair/abbey-yung-method]] · **Rules:** [[../../wiki/hair/step-rules-and-conflicts]] · **Priorities:** [[status-and-priorities]]

## Protocol

1. State **hypothesis** and **single variable** (product or step).  
2. Set **duration** (e.g. 2–3 washes or 2 weeks overnight).  
3. Log **before/after** signals: scalp (itch, grease timing), lengths (dry, tangled, frizz, heavy).  
4. Outcome: `keep` | `drop` | `retest` → update routine changelog or [[../../wiki/learnings/]].  

Journal naming: `journal/YYYY-MM-DD-hair-<short-topic>.md` (template: [[../../templates/hair-experiment]]).

## Signals cheat sheet

| Signal | Might mean |
|--------|------------|
| Greasy roots under 24 h | Shampoo too gentle or need deep-clean |
| Squeaky, itchy scalp | Too stripping — ease clarifying frequency |
| Soft in shower, dry next day | Need leave-in or overnight seal |
| Frizzy but not dry | Smoothing/technique/sealer — not more shampoo |
| Heavy, waxy lengths | Too much oil + bond + mask same week — see [[../../wiki/hair/step-rules-and-conflicts]] |
