# Hair experiments

Track **one change at a time** when tweaking the base routine. **Current wash-day base:** [[routine-wash-day]] · Products: [[../../resources/products/hair-owned]]

## Protocol

1. State **hypothesis** and **single variable** (product or step).  
2. Set **duration** (e.g. 2–3 washes or 2 weeks).  
3. Log **before/after** signals: scalp (itch, grease timing), lengths (dry, tangled, frizz, heavy).  
4. Outcome: `keep` | `drop` | `retest` → update routine changelog or [[../../wiki/learnings/]].  

Journal naming: `journal/YYYY-MM-DD-hair-<short-topic>.md` (template: [[../../templates/hair-experiment]]).

## Signals cheat sheet (your profile)

| Signal | Might mean |
|--------|------------|
| Greasy roots under 24 h | Shampoo too gentle or need deep-clean |
| Squeaky, itchy scalp | Too stripping — ease OUAI to every 2 weeks |
| Soft in shower, dry next day | Need leave-in or overnight seal |
| Frizzy but not dry | Technique/sealer after hydration stable |
| Heavy, waxy lengths (fine hair) | Too much mask + leave-in + OGX + overnight gloss — drop one layer |
