# Hair — status and priorities

**Hub:** [[_index]] · **Experiments:** [[experiments]] · **Method reference:** [[../../wiki/hair/abbey-yung-method]]

## Current read (Jun 2026)

**#1 issue (working hypothesis): hydration.** Hair looks dry when not actively moisturised overnight. Frizz / messy texture can persist even when morning hydration is improved.

## What helps (tested)

| Step | Notes |
|------|--------|
| Gloss treatment | Used overnight 2026-06-01 |
| Hydrating spray | Applied before bonnet |
| Bonnet | Sleep protection; push hair in |

**Outcome:** Much better in the morning than usual; still frizzy / messy.
