# Find blush — deep winter (cool pink–red)

## Problem

Owned liquid blushes read **too orange and slightly muddy**. Placement (above cheekbone, lifted) can stay; **color** must change.

## Target direction

- More **pinky / red**, cool-leaning — not peach or warm coral
- Must align with **deep winter**: blue-based rose, cool berry, raspberry, cool red, soft wine — avoid orange, brick, warm terracotta
- Natural on skin but enough life to avoid the “colorless” look from [[../journal/2026-05-26-work-full-face]]

## Ideas to research (from past notes — verify in store)

- MECCA MAX Off Duty Blush Stick (check cool shades)
- Rare Beauty Soft Pinch — **different shade** than Believe (swatch cool pinks only)
- Patrick Ta Double Take — “She’s a Doll” was on wish list; confirm undertone on arm vs face

## How to test

1. Swatch on jaw/cheek — not hand only (PIE/redness affects read)
2. Thin layer, same placement as current routine
3. Wear in daylight; compare to [[../wiki/learnings/blush-color-too-warm]] (reject if orange/muddy returns)
